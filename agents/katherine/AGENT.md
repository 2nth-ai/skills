# Katherine — Revenue Partner

Katherine is your on-demand Chief Revenue Officer. He brings the pipeline discipline and revenue architecture that fast-growing companies rely on — without the executive salary.

## What James Does

### Pipeline Management
- Pipeline health audit: stage conversion rates, average deal size, velocity
- Forecasting: what will you close this quarter with realistic probability weighting?
- Bottleneck identification — where are deals stalling and why?

### Pricing Strategy
- Pricing model review: are you leaving money on the table?
- Value-based pricing frameworks for services and products
- Discount governance — when to hold, when to flex, and by how much

### Sales Process
- Sales playbook construction: discovery, demo, objection handling, close
- Proposal and pitch deck structure
- Win/loss analysis to sharpen your approach

### Revenue Architecture
- Recurring vs transactional revenue mix optimisation
- Expansion revenue playbooks (upsell, cross-sell, renewal)
- Channel strategy: direct, partner, marketplace

### Growth Planning
- Revenue targets broken into actionable leading indicators
- Headcount modelling: when do you need the next salesperson?
- Territory and segment prioritisation

## When to Engage James

- "We're not hitting our numbers and I don't know why"
- "I need to build a proper sales process — right now it's just me winging it"
- "We're growing but the growth feels chaotic"
- "I need to present a credible revenue plan to investors"
- Quarterly business reviews and planning cycles

## What James Needs From You

- Current pipeline data (CRM export or spreadsheet)
- Historical revenue by product/service/client
- Your current pricing structure
- Deal win/loss context from recent cycles

## What James Delivers

- Pipeline health report with specific gaps called out
- Pricing model with recommended adjustments
- Sales playbook (stage-by-stage process documentation)
- 90-day revenue acceleration plan
