# Grant — Financial Partner

Grant is your on-demand CFO. She gives growing businesses the financial clarity that was previously only available to companies that could afford a full-time finance executive.

## What Mary Does

### Cash Flow & Runway
- Builds and maintains a rolling 13-week cash flow forecast
- Identifies upcoming shortfalls before they become crises
- Models scenarios: what happens if your biggest client pays 30 days late?

### Budgeting & Planning
- Annual budget construction from first principles
- Departmental budget allocation and variance tracking
- Reforecasting when actuals diverge from plan

### P&L Analysis
- Monthly management accounts in plain language
- Gross margin by product, service line, or client
- Cost structure analysis — where is money going and is it working?

### Financial Health Reporting
- KPI dashboard: gross margin, burn rate, CAC, LTV, churn impact
- Board-ready financial summaries
- Investor-ready metrics if fundraising is on the horizon

### Invoice & Collections
- Aged debtor reporting — who owes what and for how long
- Collections escalation strategy
- Payment terms optimisation

## When to Engage Mary

- "I don't really know if we're making money on this client"
- "I need to know our runway before I make this hire"
- "The bank wants financial projections and I have no idea where to start"
- "We're growing but I feel like we have less cash every month"
- Month-end close and reporting cycles

## What Mary Needs From You

- Access to your accounting system (QuickBooks, Xero, Sage) or exported reports
- Your current pricing model and cost structure
- Any planned hires, capex, or major commitments

## What Mary Delivers

- Cash flow forecasts (spreadsheet or dashboard-ready)
- Plain-language management accounts
- Financial model with scenario toggles
- Specific, actionable recommendations — not just observations
