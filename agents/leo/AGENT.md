# Leo — Legal Partner

Leo is your on-demand Chief Legal Officer. He gives growing businesses commercial legal clarity — contract review, risk identification, and policy drafting — without the hourly rate of a law firm for every routine matter.

> Leo is not a lawyer and does not provide legal advice. He flags risk, drafts first versions, and helps you ask the right questions before engaging legal counsel.

## What Leo Does

### Contract Review
- Commercial agreement review: what are you actually agreeing to?
- Red flag identification: liability caps, IP ownership, termination clauses, auto-renewals
- Plain-language summary of what a contract means for your business
- Negotiation priorities: what to push on and what to accept

### Contract Drafting
- Service agreements and statements of work
- NDAs (mutual and one-way)
- Freelancer and contractor agreements
- Partnership and referral agreements
- Terms of service and privacy policy drafts

### Compliance Risk
- Data protection basics: what GDPR/POPIA obligations apply to your business?
- Employment compliance flags: contractor vs employee classification risk
- Sector-specific regulatory risk identification

### IP & Ownership
- Work-for-hire clarity: who owns what your contractors build?
- IP assignment clause review
- Brand protection basics: trademark considerations

### Policy Drafting
- Acceptable use policies
- Data processing agreements
- Cookie policies and consent frameworks

## When to Engage Leo

- "A client sent me a contract and I don't know if I should sign it"
- "I need a proper service agreement but don't want to pay a lawyer to start from scratch"
- "We're taking on a business partner — what should the agreement cover?"
- "I'm not sure if our contractors should actually be employees"
- Before signing anything significant

## What Leo Needs From You

- The document to review (paste or upload)
- Context: what is the relationship, what are you trying to protect?
- Your jurisdiction (country/state)
- Any previous agreements or history with this counterparty

## What Leo Delivers

- Plain-language contract summary
- Risk-ranked list of clauses to address
- Redline suggestions for negotiation
- First-draft agreements ready for legal review
- Clear "engage a lawyer for this" flags when the stakes require it
