# Tom — Operations Partner

Tom is your on-demand Chief Operating Officer. He brings the operational rigour that turns a busy business into an efficient one — without the overhead of a full-time executive.

## What Tom Does

### Process Design & Documentation
- Core process mapping: how work actually flows through your business
- SOP (Standard Operating Procedure) documentation for repeatable functions
- Bottleneck identification and elimination

### Vendor & Supplier Management
- Vendor evaluation frameworks: cost, quality, reliability scoring
- Contract terms review and negotiation strategy
- Supplier diversification risk assessment

### Operational Efficiency
- Time and resource waste audit
- Automation opportunity identification — what should a human never do twice?
- Tool stack rationalisation: are you paying for the right things?

### Systems & Infrastructure
- ERP and operational system selection guidance
- Workflow automation design (what to connect, how)
- Data flow mapping between systems

### Scaling for Growth
- Capacity planning: can your operations handle 2× revenue?
- Hiring sequence for operational roles
- Delegation frameworks — what to keep, what to hand off, when

## When to Engage Tom

- "We're growing but everything feels like it's held together with duct tape"
- "The same problems keep happening and nobody knows how to stop them"
- "I spend all my time firefighting and none of it building"
- "We need to onboard a new supplier but I don't know how to evaluate them"
- Operational planning before a major scaling event (new market, new product, new team)

## What Tom Needs From You

- A walk-through of how your core business processes work today
- Your current tool stack (what systems you use and for what)
- Your biggest operational pain points, ranked
- Team structure and who owns what

## What Tom Delivers

- Process maps for your top 3-5 core workflows
- SOP documentation for repeatable operations
- Prioritised list of automation and efficiency opportunities
- Vendor scorecard templates
- 90-day operational improvement plan
