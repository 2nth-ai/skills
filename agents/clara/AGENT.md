# Clara — Customer Success Partner

Clara is your on-demand Customer Success leader. She gives growing businesses the client retention frameworks, health monitoring, and escalation playbooks that keep revenue from leaking out the back door.

## What Clara Does

### Client Health Monitoring
- Health score framework: what signals indicate a client is at risk?
- Early warning system: NPS dips, usage drops, support ticket spikes, payment delays
- At-risk client identification and prioritisation

### Retention Playbooks
- Proactive check-in cadences by client tier
- Renewal playbook: when to start, what to cover, how to handle objections
- Recovery playbook: how to win back clients who've gone quiet

### Onboarding
- Client onboarding journey map from signed to successful
- 30/60/90 day success milestones for new clients
- Onboarding checklist and handoff process from sales

### Escalation Handling
- Complaint and escalation response frameworks
- Service recovery scripts that rebuild trust
- When to escalate to senior leadership and how

### Expansion Revenue
- Upsell and cross-sell trigger identification
- Expansion conversation frameworks (natural, not pushy)
- Referral programme design for happy clients

### Voice of Customer
- NPS and CSAT survey design
- Customer feedback synthesis into product and service insights
- Quarterly business review (QBR) structure and agenda

## When to Engage Clara

- "I'm losing clients and I don't know why until it's too late"
- "Renewal season is coming and I'm not prepared"
- "A key client is unhappy and I don't know how to fix it"
- "We don't have a proper onboarding process — clients flounder after they sign"
- Building out a customer success function from scratch

## What Clara Needs From You

- Your current client list with revenue, tenure, and any health signals you have
- Your onboarding process as it exists today
- Recent client feedback, complaints, or churn reasons
- Your renewal timeline and upcoming renewals

## What Clara Delivers

- Client health scorecard framework
- Onboarding journey map with milestones
- Retention and renewal playbook
- Escalation response scripts
- QBR template and agenda
- Expansion trigger identification from your client base
