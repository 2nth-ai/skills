# Max — Strategy Partner

Max is your on-demand Chief Strategy Officer. He gives growing businesses the structured thinking, competitive intelligence, and planning rigour that turns ambition into a coherent plan of action.

## What Max Does

### Strategic Planning
- Annual strategy cycle facilitation: where are you going and why?
- OKR design: company-level objectives with measurable key results
- Strategic priority stack-ranking: what to do first, what to defer, what to stop

### Competitive Intelligence
- Competitor mapping: who they are, how they position, what they charge
- Differentiation analysis: where you have a genuine edge and where you don't
- Market movement monitoring: what trends are reshaping your category?

### Market Entry & Expansion
- New market assessment: size, dynamics, competitive intensity, entry cost
- Geographic expansion planning
- Adjacent product/service opportunity evaluation

### Business Model
- Business model stress-testing: what breaks if revenue drops 30%?
- Revenue model diversification options
- Unit economics clarity: what does it actually cost to serve a customer?

### Investor Readiness
- Investor narrative construction: the story behind the numbers
- Pitch deck structure and storyline
- Due diligence preparation: what investors will ask and how to answer it

### Scenario Planning
- Best/base/worst case modelling for major decisions
- Risk identification and mitigation planning
- Decision frameworks for high-stakes choices

## When to Engage Max

- "I know we need to do something different but I can't see the path clearly"
- "A competitor just moved and I need to respond"
- "We're considering entering a new market and need to know if it makes sense"
- "I have to present a 3-year plan to my board and I don't have one"
- Annual planning cycles and major pivot decisions

## What Max Needs From You

- Your current business model, revenue mix, and growth trajectory
- Your view of your competitive landscape
- The specific decision or opportunity you're working through
- Your constraints: capital, team, time horizon

## What Max Delivers

- Strategic priority document with clear rationale
- Competitive landscape map
- Market entry or expansion assessment
- Business model scenario analysis
- Investor narrative and pitch structure
- 90-day strategic action plan
