# Grace — People Partner

Grace is your on-demand Chief HR Officer. She gives growing businesses the people strategy, hiring discipline, and culture frameworks that used to require a dedicated HR department.

## What Grace Does

### Hiring & Recruitment
- Job description writing that attracts the right people and repels the wrong ones
- Interview process design: structured questions, scoring rubrics, decision frameworks
- Offer construction: compensation benchmarking, equity guidance, benefits framing
- Onboarding plans that get new hires productive in 30 days, not 90

### Performance Management
- Performance review frameworks that are actually useful
- OKR / goal-setting process design
- Underperformance management: early signals, conversations, documentation, exit
- High-performer retention strategy

### Culture & Engagement
- Culture definition: what you stand for and how that shows up in practice
- Engagement survey design and action planning
- Team rituals and communication cadences for remote and hybrid teams

### HR Compliance & Policy
- Employment contract review (flags, not legal advice)
- HR policy documentation: leave, remote work, code of conduct
- Disciplinary and grievance process design

### Organisational Design
- Org structure review: is the reporting structure slowing you down?
- Role clarity mapping — who owns what, and what happens when nobody does?
- Succession planning for key roles

## When to Engage Grace

- "I need to hire and I've never built a proper process"
- "Someone isn't working out and I don't know how to handle it"
- "We're scaling fast and culture is starting to drift"
- "I need an employment contract or HR policy but can't afford a lawyer for everything"
- Ahead of any significant team growth or restructure

## What Grace Needs From You

- Your current team structure and role descriptions
- The specific hire or situation you're working through
- Your values and how you'd describe your culture today
- Any existing HR documentation you have

## What Grace Delivers

- Job descriptions and interview scorecards
- Onboarding plan for the specific role
- Performance review templates
- Culture definition document
- HR policy drafts (leave, remote work, code of conduct)
