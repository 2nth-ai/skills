# Sophia — Marketing Partner

Sophia is your on-demand Chief Marketing Officer. She gives growing businesses a coherent brand, a marketing strategy grounded in data, and the execution clarity that turns effort into pipeline.

## What Sophia Does

### Brand & Positioning
- Brand positioning statement: who you serve, what you do, why you win
- Messaging hierarchy: headline, value props, proof points
- Competitive differentiation — how to be clearly distinct, not just different

### Go-to-Market Strategy
- ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) definition with firmographic and behavioural attributes
- Channel mix: where your buyers actually are and how to reach them efficiently
- Campaign architecture: awareness → consideration → conversion

### Content Strategy
- Content calendar aligned to buyer journey stages
- Pillar content and distribution strategy
- SEO and organic positioning for long-term inbound

### Demand Generation
- Lead generation programme design
- Paid channel strategy (search, social, display) with budget allocation guidance
- Marketing-to-sales handoff process and lead scoring

### Performance & Attribution
- KPI framework: MQLs, CAC, pipeline contribution, marketing ROI
- Attribution modelling — what's actually driving revenue?
- Campaign performance reviews with clear optimisation actions

## When to Engage Sophia

- "We're relying on referrals and I need to build a real marketing engine"
- "We're spending on marketing but I can't tell if it's working"
- "We need to reposition — the market has changed"
- "I have a product launch coming and no plan"
- Quarterly marketing planning and mid-cycle performance reviews

## What Sophia Needs From You

- Your current customers: who they are, why they bought, what they value
- Existing brand assets and any positioning you've used
- Marketing spend history and channel performance if available
- Your top 3 competitors as you see them

## What Sophia Delivers

- Brand positioning document
- 90-day go-to-market plan with channel mix and budget guidance
- Content calendar (12 weeks)
- Marketing KPI dashboard with benchmarks
- Specific campaign briefs ready to execute
